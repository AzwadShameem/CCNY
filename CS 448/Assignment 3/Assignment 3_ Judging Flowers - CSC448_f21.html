In this assignment we'll build off our previous knowledge and introduce classification models

Assignment Objectives

  • Become familiar with a real dataset

Completion of Assignment 3 - Equation of a Slime is expected. Knowledge of the basic syntax of Python is expected. As is background knowledge of the algorithms you will use in this assignment. If part of this assignment seems unclear or has an error please reach out via our course's Slack channel.

Overview

In this assignment you will be using a variety of different classification models on the (in)famous Iris Dataset. Essentially the dataset records information on the recorded attributes of three species of flowers that look quite similar.
Iris Setosa, Iris Versicolor, and Iris Virginica respectively
You can read more details about the dataset in the sklearn documentation.

Sample Notebook

Here's a template notebook to get started: Assignment 4 - Sample.
At this point, you know the drill. Follow along with the sections in the sample notebook. You will need to read through a variety of different SciKit Learn pages through the course of this assignment but it's up to you to find them.
As always post questions in Slack if you get stuck, ideally in the #general channel so that everyone can help and benefit from the answers.
Best of luck!

Submission

Just as with Assignment 2. Please make your submissions on Blackboard with a Google Colaboratory Link. Make sure that your notebook is hosted on GitHub and that your code runs to completion on Colaboratory without errors.
If you want me to be in a better mood when grading your assignment, make your submission a hyperlink as well.
